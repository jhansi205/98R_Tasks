Lord Ram

All about lord ram

Rama was born to King Dasharatha and Queen Kaushalya in Ayodhya. His birth is celebrated as Rama Navami. Rama grew up with his three half-brothers: Bharata, Lakshmana, and Shatrughna. He was known for his exemplary character, strength, and intelligence. Marriage to Sita: Rama's marriage to Sita, the daughter of King Janaka, is a significant event in his life. Rama won Sita's hand by stringing the divine bow of Lord Shiva, which no one else could lift. The couple's union is celebrated as a symbol of marital devotion and love.
